Integrate \( \int_0^1\frac{\ln^2{x}}{x^2 + 1} \; dx \)

\[ I = \int_0^1\frac{\ln^2{x}}{x^2 + 1} \; dx \] By infinite series expansion, we know that: \[ \frac{1}{x^2 + 1} = \sum_{n=0}^\infty (-1)^{n} x^{2n} \] Thus, \[ I = \int_0^1 \ln^2{x} \sum_{n=0}^\infty (-1)^{n} x^{2n} \; dx \] Switching the order of summation and integral: \[ I = \sum_{n=0}^\infty (-1)^{n} \int_0^1 \ln^2{(x)} \; x^{2n} \; dx \tag{1} \] Integrating by parts, we have: \[ \int \ln^2{(x)} \; x^{2n} \; dx = \int \ln^2{(x)} \; d(\frac{x^{2n+1}}{2n+1}) = \frac{x^{2n+1}}{2n+1} \ln^2{x} - \int \frac{x^{2n+1}}{2n+1} \; d(\ln^2{x}) \] \[ \int \ln^2{(x)} \; x^{2n} \; dx = \frac{x^{2n+1}}{2n+1} \ln^2{x} - \int \frac{x^{2n+1}}{2n+1} 2 \; \ln {(x)} \; \frac{1}{x} \; dx = \frac{x^{2n+1}}{2n+1} \ln^2{x} - \frac{2}{2n+1} \int \ln{(x)} \; x^{2n} \; dx \] Using an intermediate steps from \( \int_0^\infty\frac{\ln{x}}{x^2 + 1} \; dx \), we know that: \[ \int \ln{(x)} \; x^{2n} \; dx = \frac{x^{2n+1} \ln{x}}{2n+1} - \frac{x^{2n+1}}{(2n+1)^2} \] Hence, \[ \int \ln^2{(x)} \; x^{2n} \; dx = \frac{x^{2n+1}}{2n+1} \ln^2{x} - \frac{2}{2n+1} \Biggl( \frac{x^{2n+1} \ln{x}}{2n+1} - \frac{x^{2n+1}}{(2n+1)^2} \Biggr) \] Thus, \[ \int \ln^2{(x)} \; x^{2n} \; dx = \frac{x^{2n+1}}{2n+1} \ln^2{x} - \frac{2}{(2n+1)^2} \frac{x^{2n+1} \ln{x}}{2n+1} + \frac{2}{(2n+1)^3} x^{2n+1} \] Substituting this result in equation (1), we get: \[ I = \sum_{n=0}^\infty (-1)^{n} \Biggl[ \frac{x^{2n+1}}{2n+1} \ln^2{x} - \frac{2}{(2n+1)^2} \frac{x^{2n+1} \ln{x}}{2n+1} + \frac{2}{(2n+1)^3} x^{2n+1} \Biggr]_0^1 = \sum_{n=0}^\infty (-1)^{n} \Biggl[ (0 - 0 + \frac{2}{(2n+1)^3}) - ( 0 - 0 + 0) \Biggr] \] \[ I = 2 \sum_{n=0}^\infty (-1)^{n} \frac{1}{(2n+1)^3} \]
