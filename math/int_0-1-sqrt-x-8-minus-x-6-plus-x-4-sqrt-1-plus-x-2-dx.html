Integrate \( \int_0^1 \sqrt{x^8 - x^6 + x^4} \; \sqrt{1 + x^2} \; dx \)

\[ I = \int_0^1 \sqrt{x^8 - x^6 + x^4} \; \sqrt{1 + x^2} \; dx \] \[ I = \int_0^1 \sqrt{x^4(x^4 - x^2 + 1)} \; \sqrt{1 + x^2} \; dx = \int_0^1 x^2 \sqrt{x^4 - x^2 + 1} \; \sqrt{1 + x^2} \; dx \] \[ I = \int_0^1 x^2 \sqrt{(x^4 - x^2 + 1)(1 + x^2)} \; dx = \int_0^1 x^2 \sqrt{x^6 + 1} \; dx \] Substituting \( x^3 = t \Rightarrow 3x^2 \; dx = dt \), and changing the limits from \(x=0, x=1 \) to \( t=0, t=1 \), we get: \[ I = \int_0^1 \sqrt{t^2 + 1} \; \frac{dt}{3} = \frac{1}{3} \int_0^1 \sqrt{t^2 + 1} \; dt \] Substituting \( t = \tan \theta \Rightarrow dt = \sec^2 \theta \; d\theta \), and changing the limits from \(t=0, t=1 \) to \( \theta=0, \theta=\frac{\pi}{4} \), we get: \[ I = \frac{1}{3} \int_0^\frac{\pi}{4} \sqrt{\tan^2 \theta+ 1} \sec^2 \theta \; d\theta = \frac{1}{3} \int_0^\frac{\pi}{4} \sec^3 \theta \; d\theta \] Using \( \int \sec^3 \theta = \frac{1}{2} ( \sec \theta \tan \theta + \ln{(\sec \theta + \tan \theta)} ) \), we get: \[ I = \frac{1}{3} \Biggl( \frac{1}{2} \sec \theta \tan \theta + \frac{1}{2} \ln{(\sec \theta + \tan \theta)} \Biggr) \Biggr|_0^\frac{\pi}{4} = \frac{1}{6} \Biggl( \sec \theta \tan \theta + \ln{(\sec \theta + \tan \theta)} \Biggr) \Biggr|_0^\frac{\pi}{4} \] \[ I = \frac{1}{6} \Biggl( (\sec \frac{\pi}{4} \tan \frac{\pi}{4} + \ln{(\sec \frac{\pi}{4} + \tan \frac{\pi}{4})}) - (\sec 0 \tan 0 + \ln{(\sec 0 + \tan 0)}) \Biggr) \] \[ I = \frac{1}{6} \Biggl( (\sqrt{2} + \ln{(\sqrt{2} + 1)}) - (0 + \ln{(1 + 0)}) \Biggr) \] \[ I = \frac{1}{6} (\sqrt{2} + \ln{(\sqrt{2} + 1)}) \]
