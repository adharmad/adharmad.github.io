Integrate \( \int_0^1 x^x \; dx \):

\[ I = \int_0^1 x^x \; dx \] Using \( x^x = e^{\ln{x^x}} = e^{x\ln{x}} \), we get: \[ I = \int_0^1 e^{x\ln{x}} \; dx \] The Taylor series expansion for \( e^x \) is: \[ e^x = \sum_{n=0}^\infty \frac{x^n}{n!} \] Hence, the Taylor series expansion for \( e^{x\ln{x}} \) is: \[ e^{x\ln{x}} = \sum_{n=0}^\infty \frac{(x\ln{x})^n}{n!} \] Thus: \[ I = \int_0^1 \sum_{n=0}^\infty \frac{(x\ln{x})^n}{n!} \; dx = \sum_{n=0}^\infty \frac{1}{n!} \int_0^1 (x\ln{x})^n \; dx \] Using integration by parts we get: \[ I = \sum_{n=0}^\infty \frac{1}{n!} \Biggl( \int_0^1 (\ln{x})^n \; d(\frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1}) \Biggr) = \sum_{n=0}^\infty \frac{1}{n!} \Biggl((\ln{x})^n \frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1} \Biggr|_0^1 - \int_0^1 \frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1} \; d((\ln{x})^n) \Biggr) \] \[ I = \sum_{n=0}^\infty \frac{1}{n!} \Biggl(0 - \int_0^1 \frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1} n (\ln{x})^{n-1} \frac{1}{x} \; dx \Biggr) = \sum_{n=0}^\infty \frac{1}{n!} (-1) \Biggl(\int_0^1 \frac{n x^n}{n+1} (\ln{x})^{n-1} \; dx \Biggr) \] \[ I = \sum_{n=0}^\infty \frac{1}{n!} (-1) \frac{n}{n+1} \Biggl(\int_0^1 x^n (\ln{x})^{n-1} \; dx \Biggr) \] \[ I = \sum_{n=0}^\infty \frac{1}{n!} (-1) \frac{n}{n+1} \Biggl(\int_0^1 (\ln{x})^{n-1} \; d(\frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1}) \Biggr) = \sum_{n=0}^\infty \frac{1}{n!} (-1) \frac{n}{n+1} \Biggl( (\ln{x})^{n-1} \frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1} \Biggr|_0^1 - \int_0^1 \frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1} \; d((\ln{x})^{n-1}) \Biggr) \] \[ I = \sum_{n=0}^\infty \frac{1}{n!} (-1) \frac{n}{n+1} \Biggl(0 - \int_0^1 \frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1} (n-1) (\ln{x})^{n-2} \frac{1}{x} \; dx \Biggr) \] \[ I = \sum_{n=0}^\infty \frac{1}{n!} (-1)^2 \frac{n(n-1)}{(n+1)^2} \Biggl(\int_0^1 x^n (\ln{x})^{n-2} \; dx \Biggr) \] Applying integration by parts repeatedly k times, we get: \[ I = \sum_{n=0}^\infty \frac{1}{n!} (-1)^k \frac{n(n-1)(n-2)...(n-k+1)}{(n+1)^k} \Biggl(\int_0^1 x^n (\ln{x})^{n-k} \; dx \Biggr) \] Setting k=n to make the logarithmic term disappear: \[ I = \sum_{n=0}^\infty \frac{1}{n!} (-1)^n \frac{n!}{(n+1)^n} \Biggl(\int_0^1 x^n \; dx \Biggr) = \sum_{n=0}^\infty \frac{(-1)^n}{(n+1)^n} \Biggl(\int_0^1 x^n \; dx \Biggr) \] \[ I = \sum_{n=0}^\infty \frac{(-1)^n}{(n+1)^n} \Biggl(\frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1} \Biggr|_0^1 \Biggr) = \sum_{n=0}^\infty \frac{(-1)^n}{(n+1)^n} \Biggl(\frac{1 - 0}{n+1} \Biggr) \] \[ I = \sum_{n=0}^\infty \frac{(-1)^n}{(n+1)^{n+1}} \]
