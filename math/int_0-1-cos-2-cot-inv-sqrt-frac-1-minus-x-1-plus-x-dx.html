Integrate \( \int_0^1 \cos( 2 \cot^{-1} \sqrt{\frac{1-x}{1+x}}) \; dx \)

\[ I = \int_0^1 \cos( 2 \cot^{-1} \sqrt{\frac{1-x}{1+x}}) \; dx \] Substituting \( x = \cos \theta \Rightarrow dx = -\sin \theta \; d\theta \), and changing the limits from \(x=0, x=1 \) to \( \theta=\frac{\pi}{2}, \theta =0 \), we get: \[ I = \int_\frac{\pi}{2}^0 \cos \Biggl( 2 \cot^{-1} \sqrt{\frac{1-\cos \theta}{1+\cos \theta}} \Biggr) \; (-\sin \theta) \; d\theta = \int_0^\frac{\pi}{2} \cos \Biggl( 2 \cot^{-1} \sqrt{\frac{2 \sin^2 \frac{\theta}{2}}{2 \cos^2 \frac{\theta}{2}}} \Biggr) \; \sin \theta \; d\theta \] \[ I = \int_0^\frac{\pi}{2} \cos ( 2 \cot^{-1} \sqrt{\tan^2 \frac{\theta}{2}} ) \; \sin \theta \; d\theta = \int_0^\frac{\pi}{2} \cos ( 2 \cot^{-1} (\tan \frac{\theta}{2})) \; \sin \theta \; d\theta \] \[ I = \int_0^\frac{\pi}{2} \cos ( 2 \cot^{-1} (\cot ( \frac{\pi}{2} - \frac{\theta}{2}))) \; \sin \theta \; d\theta = \int_0^\frac{\pi}{2} \cos ( 2 (\frac{\pi}{2} - \frac{\theta}{2})) \; \sin \theta \; d\theta \] \[ I = \int_0^\frac{\pi}{2} \cos (\pi - \theta) \; \sin \theta \; d\theta = - \int_0^\frac{\pi}{2} \cos \theta \sin \theta \; d\theta \] \[ I = - \frac{1}{2} \int_0^\frac{\pi}{2} \sin 2\theta \; d\theta = - \frac{1}{2} \frac{\cos 2\theta}{2} \Biggr|_0^\frac{\pi}{2} \] \[ I = - \frac{1}{4} (\cos \pi - \cos 0) = - \frac{1}{4} (-1-1) \] \[ I = \frac{1}{2} \]
